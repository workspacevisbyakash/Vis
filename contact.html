Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to keep your website or application running smoothly. This includes security updates, bug fixes, and performance optimization.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We can work with your existing brand guidelines, style guides, and design systems to ensure consistency across all your digital touchpoints.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Typically, we work with milestone-based payments: 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint, and 25% upon completion.
